# Billing Policies and Guidelines

This document contains bank policies related to billing, fees, transactions, and financial operations. It is used by the support system to provide accurate and consistent responses.

---

## 1. Monthly Maintenance Fees

* Accounts may incur a monthly maintenance fee if they do not meet minimum balance or activity requirements.
* Fees may be waived if the customer:

  * Maintains a minimum balance
  * Has qualifying deposits or transactions
* Fees are applied automatically at the end of the billing cycle.

---

## 2. Transaction Disputes and Unauthorized Charges

* Customers should report any unrecognized or unauthorized transactions immediately.
* These cases are considered **high priority** and may require human intervention.
* The system should NOT attempt to resolve fraud cases automatically.
* Always escalate when:

  * The user explicitly says they did not authorize a transaction
  * There are multiple suspicious transactions

---

## 3. Refund Processing

* Refunds depend on the merchant and payment method.
* Typical processing time:

  * 3–5 business days after the refund is issued
* Delays may occur depending on external systems.

---

## 4. Card Declines

Common reasons for declined transactions:

* Insufficient funds
* Security checks triggered
* Expired card
* Daily or transaction limits exceeded

Support guidance:

* Suggest checking balance and card details
* If unclear, recommend contacting support

---

## 5. Transaction Reversals

* Transaction reversals are NOT handled automatically.
* These require manual review by a human agent.
* Always escalate requests that include:

  * "reverse this transaction"
  * "cancel this payment"

---

## 6. Transfer Issues

* Internal transfers are usually instant.
* External transfers may take 1–3 business days.
* Delays may occur due to:

  * Bank processing times
  * Weekends or holidays

Escalation guidance:

* Escalate if:

  * Transfer delay exceeds expected timeframe significantly
  * Large amounts are involved

---

## 7. Duplicate Charges

* Duplicate charges may occur due to:

  * Merchant processing errors
  * Temporary authorization holds
* If charges persist after a few days, further investigation may be required.

Escalation guidance:

* Escalate if:

  * Duplicate charges remain after expected resolution window

---

## 8. Fees Transparency

* All fees should be documented in the account terms and conditions.
* Customers can review fee details in their account statements.

Support guidance:

* Provide general explanation
* Avoid making guarantees without checking account-specific details

---

## General Rules for Support Agent

* NEVER fabricate billing policies
* ALWAYS prefer escalation for:

  * fraud-related issues
  * transaction reversals
  * unclear or high-risk financial situations
* Use cautious and precise language when discussing financial matters

---

## Tone Guidelines

* Be clear and professional
* Avoid blaming the customer
* Provide actionable next steps when possible
