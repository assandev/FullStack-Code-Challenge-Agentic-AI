# General Support Information

This document contains general information about the bank's services, policies, and non-technical inquiries.

---

## 1. Customer Support Availability

Description:
Information about when and how users can get help.

### Support Guidance
- Customer support is available 24/7 via chat and phone
- Some specialized services may have limited hours

---

## 2. Bank Transfers

Description:
Information about transfer timing and behavior.

### Support Guidance
- Internal transfers: usually instant
- External transfers: 1–3 business days
- Delays may occur on weekends or holidays

### Escalation Signals
- Transfers delayed beyond expected timeframe
- High-value transfers not completed

---

## 3. Updating Personal Information

Description:
Users may want to update profile details.

### Support Guidance
- Direct users to account settings
- Inform that some changes require verification

### Escalation Signals
- User cannot update critical information
- Verification repeatedly fails

---

## 4. Account Features and Services

Description:
General questions about what the bank offers.

### Support Guidance
- Provide high-level explanation of services
- Suggest checking app or website for detailed info

---

## 5. Security and Best Practices

Description:
Guidance on keeping accounts secure.

### Support Guidance
- Never share passwords or OTPs
- Enable security features if available
- Report suspicious activity immediately

### Escalation Signals
- User reports possible security breach
- Suspicious access or activity mentioned

---

## 6. Fees and Policies (General Awareness)

Description:
Users asking general questions about fees.

### Support Guidance
- Explain that fees depend on account type and usage
- Refer to billing policies when needed

---

## General Rules for Support Agent

- Keep answers simple and informative
- Avoid unnecessary technical detail
- Redirect to billing or technical flows if needed
- Ask clarifying questions if the request is vague

---

## Tone Guidelines

- Friendly and informative
- Clear and concise
- Avoid overcomplicating explanations
